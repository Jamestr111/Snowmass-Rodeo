How to enter the Wednesday Night Rodeo.

Entries

Call it in on Tuesday

Entries are taken by phone at (970) 923‑9270, Tuesday between 6:00 and 8:00 PM, for that week's Wednesday Night Rodeo.

Pre‑entries for future rodeos can also be made using the sign‑up sheet available from the Rodeo Secretary at the end of each rodeo. All entry fees must be paid in full before the start of the rodeo, or the contestant will be withdrawn from competition.

Checks for entry fees should be made out to SWHA (Snowmass Western Heritage Association).

Call to enter

Tuesdays, 6:00–8:00 PM, for the following night's rodeo.
