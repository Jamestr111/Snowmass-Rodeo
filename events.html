What's in the arena

Rodeo action starts at 7:00pm and runs about 90 minutes. Here's what you'll see, event by event.

Opening

Grand Entry

Cowboys and cowgirls ride into the arena carrying flags in tribute to the American Flag — the traditional kickoff to every performance.

Rough Stock

Bareback Riding

Trickier than it looks — with nothing to hold but a suitcase‑like handle on a strap, the rider must stay balanced and coordinated with a powerful horse for a full eight seconds.

Timed Team Event

Team Roping

Partnership, precise timing, and anticipation. The header ropes the steer's horns and turns it left, giving the heeler a shot at its hind legs — rodeo's only true team event.

Rough Stock

Saddle Bronc Riding

Known as "rodeo's classic event." Rooted in breaking saddle horses, it combines strength, style and rhythm — the rider holds a single thick rein and is disqualified for touching the horse or himself with his free hand.

Family Team Event

Dally Ribbon Roping

A roper on horseback catches the calf and dallies the rope, then a runner on foot collects the ribbon from the calf's tail and races it back to the box. Teams must include one male and one female contestant; kids under 13 may enter.

Fan Favorite

Barrel Racing

Three barrels, one horse, one rider, and a cloverleaf pattern to run clean. Timed to the hundredth of a second.

Most Dangerous

Bull Riding

2,000+ pounds of bull, one flat braided rope, one hand free. The most dangerous event in rodeo — and the one with the fewest rules. Eight seconds, no touching yourself or the bull with your free hand.

Added 2023

Breakaway Roping

Blink and you'll miss it. A timed ladies' event and a style of calf roping without tying — the roper throws a lasso around the calf's neck as fast as possible after it breaks from the chute.

For The Little Buckaroos

Kids' Events

Sign‑up happens on the grounds between 5:30pm and 7pm, right before the rodeo starts. A parent or legal guardian must be present to sign a liability release.

Ages 4–10

Calf Scramble

Kids line up in the arena and chase calves wearing ribboned tails, trying to pull a ribbon free. First three to turn one in win awards; everyone who competes gets a prize. No entry limit — sign up before 7pm.

Free · Ages 4–7

Mutton Bustin'

The crowd favorite: little buckaroos hang onto a woolly sheep for eight seconds. Open to kids under 50 lbs. Day‑of entry only — eight contestants are drawn at random from the sign‑up pool.
