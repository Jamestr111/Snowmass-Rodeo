Sponsorship

The Snowmass Rodeo is produced entirely by the Snowmass Western Heritage Association — sponsors keep it running.

Who We Are

Snowmass Western Heritage Association

SWHA is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit whose mission is to inform and educate visitors and area residents about the western heritage and character of Snowmass Village and the Roaring Fork Valley, through events and education.

SWHA produces the Snowmass Rodeo, a summer series every Wednesday evening, June 17 through August 19. 2026 marks our 52nd year. Fundraising supports scholarship opportunities for rodeo students and donations to western‑related organizations in the area.
